Scrambled Eggs:
2 eggs, 1 slice of American cheese, salt, pepper 

1. Crack both of the eggs into a bowl.
2. Stir the eggs until they become a uniform yellow mix.
3. Tear up even pieces of cheese into the mix.
4. Add salt and pepper 
5. Pour mixture on a pan on high heat.
6. Progressively turn the mixture around as it solidifies, making sure little sticks to the pan, progressively decrease heat.
7. When the mix is fluffy it is ready to be served.
